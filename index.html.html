Dealing with Workplace Stress

Posted on May 1, 2025

Someone asked about managing workplace stress. Here's my advice: It's important to set boundaries at work and make time for self-care. Try taking short breaks during the day, practice mindfulness, and disconnect completely during your time off. Remember that your mental health should always be a priority.

Building Confidence

Posted on April 28, 2025

A message came in about building self-confidence. My response: Confidence comes from a combination of self-acceptance and competence. Start by recognizing your strengths, set small achievable goals, celebrate your wins (even the small ones), and practice positive self-talk. Remember that confidence is built gradually through consistent action.
